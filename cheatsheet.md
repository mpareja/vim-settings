# Cheatsheet

A few commands that I tend to forget about:

## Unimpaired

`yob`	'background' (dark is off, light is on)
`yoc`	'cursorline'
`yod`	'diff' (actually |:diffthis| / |:diffoff|)
`yoh`	'hlsearch'
`yoi`	'ignorecase'
`yol`	'list'       (show tabs and newlines, etc.)
`yon`	'number'
`yor`	'relativenumber'
`yos`	'spell'
`you`	'cursorcolumn'
`yov`	'virtualedit'
`yow`	'wrap'
`yox`	'cursorline' 'cursorcolumn' (x as in crosshairs)
